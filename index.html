About me

I am currently pursuing a diploma in Computer Programmer Analyst at George Brown College (expected graduation in April 2021). At George Brown, I am also a Peer Tutor (GBC MobiHelp Program) where I provide academic support to students in one-on-one or small group tutoring sessions.
